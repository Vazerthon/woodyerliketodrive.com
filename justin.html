At WoodyerLiketoDrive, I Justin Woodyer am a firm believer in making lessons enjoyable. If your enjoying what your doing you are more likely to pick things up quickly, thus saving you money in the longer term.

I am a relaxed, friendly instructor who, because every person is individual, will adapt teaching methods to match your needs. I operate completely independently which means there are no restraints in how you want to learn; if you’re looking for just weekly lessons then that’s no problem. If you have a specific target in mind and need a more intensive course to attain the target, then we can work together to reach your goals. Have a look at my prices page to see what would be best for you.

Outside of work I am an active person who enjoys various activites ranging from golf, football and skiing. I have lived in Penicuik for my whole life and know all the surrounding areas well including, Peebles, West Linton, Loanhead, Dalkeith and Muselburgh. I cover all areas of Midlothian.
As Penicuik is situated perfectly in between the three local test areas of Peebles, Musselburgh and Currie, I am familiar with all the areas and roads that get used for your test when it comes around.
